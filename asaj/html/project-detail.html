More About the Product

We are proud stockists of High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE) fittings, adhering to various international standards. Our extensive range of HDPE products includes pipes and fittings, available in sizes starting from 20mm and above. In addition to this, we supply HDPE Butt Fusion and Electrofusion Welding Machines, along with essential accessories.

HDPE fittings are renowned for their durability, chemical resistance, and flexibility, making them ideal for a wide range of applications, including water supply, gas distribution, sewage systems, and industrial processes. Our fittings include but are not limited to elbows, tees, reducers, and end caps, designed to ensure leak-proof connections and long-lasting performance.

We also offer HDPE segmented fittings and saddles, providing versatile solutions for various pipeline configurations. As specialists in custom fabrications, we excel in producing bespoke headers, spools, anti-vortex plates, and puddle flanges tailored to meet specific customer requirements.

Our commitment to quality and innovation ensures that all our products meet stringent industry standards, providing reliable and efficient solutions for your piping needs.
